# IRA Google Sheets schema

## Tab 1: Event Config

Columns:

`event_id`, `active`, `event_name_ne`, `event_name_en`, `affected_province_ne`, `affected_province_en`, `affected_districts_ne`, `affected_districts_en`, `expected_palika_ids`, `expected_palikas_ne`, `expected_palikas_en`, `deaths_threshold`, `displaced_households_threshold`, `water_disruption_palikas_threshold`, `shelter_need_households_threshold`, `refresh_seconds`

Use one active row at a time. The dashboard and Google Form dropdown are scoped from `expected_palika_ids`; if that is blank, Apps Script falls back to the affected districts.

## Tab 2: Submissions

One row per palika submission. Columns map directly to the IRA form fields plus `submission_type`, `is_proxy`, `duplicate_status`, and `override_duplicate`.

`is_proxy` is `TRUE` when DEOC enters the report from a paper photo or voice call.

## Tab 3: Palika Master

Full 753-palika master list with district and province mapping.

Generated by `scripts/fetch_palika_master.py` from open Wikipedia tables that cite MoFAGA/local-level data:

- `Wards and electoral divisions of Nepal`
- `List of cities in Nepal`
- `List of gaunpalikas of Nepal`

## Tab 4: Mock Data

Twenty event-scoped submissions for `karnali-eq-2083-drill`, with 25 expected palikas and 5 outstanding palikas for tracker demonstration.
